Summer Mindfulness Retreat 2025

Reconnect with yourself in nature

August 30, 2025 • Murree, Pakistan

About the Retreat

The Summer Mindfulness Retreat 2025 is your chance to step away from the rush of daily life and immerse yourself in peace, nature, and self-discovery. Over two calming days in the serene hills of Murree, you’ll be guided through meditation, yoga, and mindful living practices designed to help you recharge your body and mind.

Highlights

Guided Meditation Sessions

Learn to quiet your mind and focus on the present moment.

Nature Walks & Outdoor Yoga

Reconnect with nature while improving flexibility and balance.

Mindfulness Workshops

Gain practical tools to reduce stress and bring calm into everyday life.
